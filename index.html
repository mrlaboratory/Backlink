What are website backlinks? (What Is Backlink)


Simply put, backlinks are one of the most important part of search engine optimization (SEO) and to increase the domain authority, search engine preference and search engine ranking of your blog, we need to create backlinks to the website.


Now if I explain the matter of backlinks to you well, then it may take a while to understand.


Backlinks mean that your website's "URL link" is on another website. And, from that website you get an external link for your website.


For example,

Suppose I am writing an article on my blog. And, in a part of my article, I gave you a "Article URL Link" to your blog.


What will happen is that you will get a quality backlink from my blog or website through the URL link given in the article on your blog or website.


And, in this way, when other websites or blogs through different mediums have the URL address of your own blog, then each of those external links will be the backlink of your website.


Then, through those backlinks or external links, Google bots pass the link juice to your website.


As a result, Google's trust in your blog grows and later the website's Google search ranking becomes better.

How many types of backlinks and what are they? (Types of backlinks)


Below, I will tell you about some types and differences of backlinks. In this way, you will be able to know the matter of backlinks more clearly.


  Internal links: 

Internal links are backlinks within our own blog. That is, when we write articles on a blog, we can create internal backlinks with links to some other articles on our blog.

    External links: 

As I said before, you have to create this type of backlinks by going to another blog or website. I mean, hope link from other website to our website.

    Link juice: 

When a backlink is created through a hyper link on another blog or website, Google bots follow those links and pass the link juice to your blog. This link juice helps to increase search engine traffic by increasing the domain authority of your blog.

     Low - quality links:

 When a backlink comes to your website from a bad, spam, low quality or harvested website, low quality links are created. These types of backlinks are very harmful for your blog.

    High - quality links: 

When a good and high quality website whose DA and PA are very good, comes backlink to your blog, then that backlink is high quality external link.

    No - follow links:

 When the "rel = nofollow" tag is used in the expected backlinks from other websites, then those backlinks are no follow backlinks. Google bot does not pass their link juice through this type of link. So, these types of backlinks do not work particularly well.

    Do - follow links: 

When the “rel = nofollow” tag is not used in the hopeful links from other websites in our blog, then those links are do - follow links. And, through this kind of link Google passes its link juice. So, this kind of backlink is very useful for your blog.


So, now you may have a good understanding of the type of backlinks. Now we will learn below "How to create backlinks for your website".

How to create backlinks? (How To Create Backlinks)


There are many rules for creating backlinks. But, there are many things you need to consider when creating backlinks for a website.


For example, do not take backlinks from any website with low quality and low domain authority.


All the time, get backlinks from websites with good quality and good domain authority.


Moreover, if you generate a large number of backlinks at once, Google may consider your blog as spam.


In this way, your Google search ranking will go down a lot. And, Google can remove your website from its search forever.


So, always try to create backlinks from high quality and clean websites with good domain authority.


To check the authority of the website, you can go to this website - "Check website authority".

5 rules to build backlinks safely


Using the methods below, I create backlinks for my own blog. And, you can also use these ways to create backlinks if you want.

1. Quora question & answer site


Quora is a question & answer website where anyone can answer people's questions. And, with the answers you have given, you can provide links to your blog and website.


Quora, a very high quality, conventional and popular website whose domain authority is 93, which is much more.


And, it will be much more profitable to get backlinks to your website from quora.


Simply search for quora questions related to your blog article and then answer that question with the URL link of your article.


This way, it is much easier to create quality backlinks for the blog. This is my favorite way to build backlinks.

2. Blog commenting


Blog commenting is the easiest way to create a backlink using the medium. And, this medium is used by almost every blogger today.


All you have to do here is find some other blogs related to the subject of your blog article (you can find it by searching on Google).


Then, find 5 to 6 blogs and go to them and comment on any of their articles.


When commenting, you need to add the URL Address of your blog to that comment and submit the comment.


In many blogs, you are asked to provide your website address when commenting. In that case, you must give the address of your blog.


Now, after submitting the comment, when that comment will be shown in the comment section of that article, you will get backlinks for your website.


This way, you can create backlinks by using the compact options at the bottom of various blog articles.


But, remember, only comment on blogs or websites that are related to your blog article.


And, never comment on “low quality” or “spammy” websites.

3. Directory submission sites


Until recently, this process of creating backlinks was very common. However, very few people are using this medium today.


In this process, you have to submit the URL address of your blog or website to various web directory websites.


Web directory websites contain thousands of different types of websites. Links and details of different websites are given as different categories.


And so, the backlinks expected from web directories are much less likely to be of good quality.


But, if you use some good web directories, which are good quality, spam free and have a lot of DA, then this process of creating a good amount of high quality backlinks is still the best.

 4. Guest posting


Guest posting is the best and most profitable way to build backlinks for a website.


Because, through guest posting you will get contextual backlinks and the quality of those links is the best.


"Guest posting" means writing and publishing articles on the blogs or websites of others related to your blog.


And, when writing an article on another's blog, you have to give the URL link of your blog in that article.


In this way, you will get a good quality backlink from that blog to your own blog.


As such, you do not need to use this process much. In a month, guest posting on 2 to 3 high quality blogs will do.


But, in the blog where you want to do guest posting, make sure that the blog has more DA and PA.


Moreover, you will do guest posting on the blog related to the niche or topic of your blog.


After looking at everything, contact the blog and website owner, you need to get permission for guest posting.


With permission, you can create an article on their blog and create a backlink for your blog with a link to it.

5. Social networking site profiles


This medium is very useful for getting good quality backlinks for the website. Simply, you just have to go to some popular and good social networking websites and create a profile.


And, when creating a profile, there is an option to link to your own website in almost every social media website.


Lastly, when you link to your website on various social media sites like YouTube, Facebook or Twitter, you get a backlink from there.


Moreover, various social networking sites, when creating a profile, you are asked to write something about yourself (about or bio).


In your bio or about me section, you can also earn backlink with the URL link of your blog.
