        How to find specific websites to create backlinks




        We need good quality websites to create backling. But where can we find this website?


        Now of course we are thinking of search engines such as: - Google, Yahoo, Bing etc. If so, you are a genius. However, there are some problems here and there is Technic.


        What do we usually do?

        If we want to find a forum related to technology, then maybe we will go to the search engine and write "All Technology Forum List", but to be honest, it will not meet your needs or good results.

        # Forum Site Search Technic:

        Enter your KEYWORD in place of KEYWORDS, for example: I will search for Forum on Technology, then I will search in Technoloy instead of KEYWORD.

        1. . KEYWORDS “powered by phpbb” inurl: / forum
        2. . KEYWORDS “powered by vbulletin” inurl: / forum

        4. . KEYWORDS “powered by smf” site: / forum

        6. . KEYWORDS “powered by phpbb” inurl: / community

        8. . KEYWORDS “powered by vbulletin” inurl: / community

        10. . KEYWORDS “powered by smf” inurl: / community

        12. . Keyword "powered by phpbb" inurl: viewtopic.php? F =

        14. . Keyword "powered by vBulletin" inurl: showthread.php

        16. . Keyword "Powered By MyBB" inurl: showthread.php? Tid =

        18. . Keyword "powered by Vanilla" inurl: discussion

        20. . Keyword "Powered By ExpressionEngine" inurl: / viewthread /

        22. . Keyword "powered by SMF" inurl: index.php / topic

        24. . Keyword "powered by YaBB" inurl: num =

        #Guest Blogging Site Search Tecnic:


        1. Keyword "Contribute to this site" OR "Add Content" OR "Become a contributor"

        2. Keyword “this is a guest post by” OR “contributing writer”

        3. Keyword “want to write for” OR “contribute to our site”

        4. Keyword “looking for guest posts” OR “guest poster wanted” OR “guest posts wanted”

        6. Keyword “become a guest blogger” OR “submit blog post” OR “submit your content” OR “submit guest post”

        7. Keyword inurl: category / guest "Submit" OR "Contribute"

        8. Keyword “my posts on other blogs” OR “my guest blogs” OR “posts on other blogs” OR “I’ve been featured on”
